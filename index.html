About Me

About Me

A huge computer nerd, is what a lot of my friends would describe me as. Hi! Welcome to my portfolio! My parents found it difficult to keep me away from a computer since I was a child. It started with playing video games, but, slowly my passion for building computer applications and games grew. It was a no-brainer for me to pursue a bachelor’s degree in computer sciences. Following a fruitful (and somewhat excruciating) four year program, I began working as a full stack software engineer. And since then, I’ve never looked back.


My specialities include problem solving, quickly learning new skills and technologies, domain driven design, and writing clean and reusable code. So far I have experience coding in Play framework with Java, Laravel framework with PHP and Javascript, VueJS, Visual C# MVC, HTML, CSS, jQuery and Bootstrap and working with technologies like MySQL, ElasticSearch, MongoDB, Jenkins and Git. I’m currently learning ReactJS, NodeJS, ExpressJS and Python. I’m still enthusiastic about learning any new technologies and sate my ever hungry knowledge appetite! Please feel free to connect with me on LinkedIn for a quick chat.
